LET'S GET STARTED!


EMOJI-ON is like a cheerful friend who helps us explore the emotions twinkling through the words of our favorite tales. It's here to share 6 basic emotions that is universally known as stated by Paul Ekman: the joy that makes us dance,the sadness that makes us strong,the fear that makes us courageous,the anger that makes us brave,the disgust that makes us appreciate the good stuff, and the surprise that makes us curious

Our mission at EMOJI-ON is simple: to support parents and teachers in fostering children's emotional development. We believe that storybooks not only aids in language acquisition but also lays the foundation of emotional development for children.

EMOJI-ON is not just for kids - it's a fantastic tool for parents and teachers too! Dive into the stories together, discuss the emotions, and create moments that sparkle in your hearts.

Whether you're a young explorer seeking the heart of a story or a parent eager to embark on emotional journeys with your little ones, EMOJI-ON is your joyful companion. Let the emotions unfold, and let the tales be a vibrant kaleidoscope of feelings!

How Does it Work?

Simply type or upload in the the story, and EMOJI-ON will work its magic, revealing the emotions within. It's like having a special emotion compass to navigate through the feelings woven into each story. We understand that every reader is unique, and so are their preferences for exploring emotions in stories. That's why we offer two convenient options to suit your reading style:

PARAGRAPH
EMOJI-ON delves deep into the emotional landscape of each paragraph, providing insights into the specific emotions present at every paragraph at a time.

WHOLE Prefer to see the bigger picture? Choose the Whole option, and EMOJI-ON will captures the essence of the narrative as a whole.

Whether you opt for the detailed insights of the Paragraph option or the panoramic view of the
          Whole option, EMOJI-ON empowers you to explore emotions in stories your way.
                    So, dive in, choose your adventure, and let the emotions unfold!

About Us

"Today a reader, tommorow a leader"

This website aims to avoid children from reading a book that has intense emotion that can harm their mental development.

Mission

To advance the field of emotion recognition by leveraging machine learning techniques, specifically Support Vector Machines (SVMs), to predict emotions in Filipino children's storybooks.

Vission

The vision of the study is to develop a robust and accurate model that can effectively analyze and predict emotional expressions in Filipino children's storybooks through the application of Support Vector Machines.
