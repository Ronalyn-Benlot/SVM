About Us

"Today a reader, tommorow a leader"

This website aims to avoid children from reading a book that has intense emotion that can harm their mental development.

Mission

To advance the field of emotion recognition by leveraging machine learning techniques, specifically Support Vector Machines (SVMs), to predict emotions in Filipino children's storybooks.

Vission

The vision of the study is to develop a robust and accurate model that can effectively analyze and predict emotional expressions in Filipino children's storybooks through the application of Support Vector Machines.
